RMO Handout Series

I am in the prcoess of developing a series of handouts intended to be used as teaching aids for RMOs rotating through ID rotations. Where I work the standard RMO rotation is ten weeks, so I leave the tenth week topic up to the residents. If they can't come up with a topic they want to learn about, we talk abour Leprosy (we have a few patients on our books).

  • 01 - Antibiotics
  • 02 - Staphylococcus aureus
  • 03 - Infective Endocarditis
  • 04 - Bone and Joint Infections
  • 05 - CNS Infections
  • 06 - Tuberculosis
  • 07 - HIV and Sexually Transmitted Infections
  • 08 - Antifungals, Antivirals, and Antiparasitics
  • 09 - Other Important Infections
  • 10 - Leprosy
